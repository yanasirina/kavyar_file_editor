Title: Old Town Fashion

Description: Editorial photography took place in the old town of Biysk in the building of the merchants Vtorov passage. In Soviet times, there was a hotel "Delovoy Dvor" in this building. Nicholas Roerich stayed here during his expedition. At that time, he was in the city as an American scientist because of the conspiracy from the Soviet government.
